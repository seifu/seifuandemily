Groom

Emily and Seifu met during their sophomore year of college at the University of Michigan. After 8 years of dating and 4 years of living in separate cities, they are looking forward to getting married and starting a life together.

Getting Here

For those of you who are traveling in from out-of-town, we are so grateful that you are making the trip to Ann Arbor for our wedding celebration. We recommend flying into the Detroit Metropolitan Airport (DTW), which is located approximately 30 minutes from Ann Arbor. Renting a car is likely the cheapest and most flexible option; however, you can also reserve a cab or shuttle service.
